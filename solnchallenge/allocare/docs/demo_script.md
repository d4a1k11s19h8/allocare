# AlloCare — Demo Video Script
## 3-Minute Timed Script

---

### [0:00 – 0:12] — THE HOOK

**[On camera: Hold up a stack of printed paper surveys]**

> "This is how most Indian NGOs collect community needs. Two hundred surveys about food insecurity in Dharavi. Collected in January. Three weeks later — not one family has received help, because nobody had time to analyze the paper."

---

### [0:12 – 0:30] — THE PROBLEM

**[Cut to: split-screen of WhatsApp messages, paper files, a spreadsheet]**

> "Local community groups collect critical data — but it's scattered. Paper forms. WhatsApp threads. Disconnected spreadsheets. No system aggregates it to show where needs are most urgent — and no system connects that data to volunteers who can help."

---

### [0:30 – 0:40] — INTRODUCE ALLOCARE

**[Title card: AlloCare logo + tagline]**

> "AlloCare is an AI-powered platform that closes the full loop — from scattered paper data to the right volunteer, deployed to the right place, in under 60 seconds."

---

### [0:40 – 1:20] — DEMO: THE MONEY SHOT 💰

**[Screen recording: AlloCare dashboard open]**

> "Let me show you. I'm an NGO coordinator. I click 'Upload Report' and photograph this paper survey."

**[Upload photo → show OCR → show Gemini processing animation]**

> "In under 10 seconds: Google Cloud Vision OCR extracts the text. Gemini 2.0 Flash analyzes it — extracting issue type, severity, affected count. Our urgency formula scores this as CRITICAL: 91 out of 100."

**[Watch the heatmap — new red marker appears]**

> "Watch the heatmap — a new red marker just appeared on Dharavi, live."

**[Click the need card → show volunteer match panel]**

> "AlloCare instantly shows me the top 3 matched volunteers: Priya, 2.3km away, Medical First Aid certified, available now. One click to assign."

---

### [1:20 – 1:50] — DEMO: VOLUNTEER SIDE

**[Switch to mobile view / volunteer perspective]**

> "Meanwhile, on the volunteer's phone — Priya gets a push notification: 'CRITICAL Need Near You.' She opens AlloCare and sees: 'Your food distribution skills will help ~47 families in Dharavi avoid hunger tonight.'"

> "She accepts, gets Google Maps directions. When she arrives, she checks in via GPS. After completion, an animated impact scorecard shows her the families she helped, her new badges, and her community rank."

> "Research shows this impact framing increases volunteer retention by 40 percent."

---

### [1:50 – 2:20] — ARCHITECTURE + GOOGLE TECHNOLOGIES

**[Architecture diagram slide]**

> "Under the hood: Cloud Vision for paper OCR. Gemini 2.0 Flash for urgency intelligence. Google Maps with heatmap visualization. Distance Matrix API for real-world proximity matching. Firebase Firestore for live data sync. Cloud Functions for serverless processing. And Firebase Cloud Messaging for instant volunteer alerts."

---

### [2:20 – 2:45] — IMPACT + RESEARCH GROUNDING

**[Stats slide]**

> "This is grounded in research. A 2025 study of 65 NGOs showed AI reduces data entry by 60% and resource allocation time by 30%. We're operationalizing exactly that finding."

> "AlloCare addresses SDG 1 — No Poverty, SDG 10 — Reduced Inequalities, and SDG 17 — Partnerships for the Goals. Our target: 1,000 NGOs in India using the free tier within 12 months."

---

### [2:45 – 3:00] — CLOSE

**[Team photo + GitHub/demo URL on screen]**

> "AlloCare. Free for NGOs. Powered by Google AI. From paper survey to deployed volunteer — in under 60 seconds. The link to our live demo is at the top of our GitHub readme. Thank you."

---
